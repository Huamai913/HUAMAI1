About HUAMAI

HUAMAI is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Canadian expertise in architectural design, art therapy, environmental sustainability, and educational innovation on a global scale. We aim to leverage the talents of Canadian professionals to drive cultural preservation, environmental stewardship, and equitable education through impactful global projects, thereby enhancing Canadaâ€™s influence on the world.

Core Objectives

  • Cultural Preservation: Develop and implement innovative architectural solutions for cultural heritage conservation, blending Canadian traditional and modern design elements.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Promote international green building practices and low-carbon infrastructure, including wetland restoration and renewable energy systems.
  • Educational Innovation: Design modern educational facilities and deliver international courses to promote equitable access to quality education.

Core Values

Connection

Fostering deep connections between humans, AI, and diverse cultures.

Love

Approaching all projects and beneficiaries with care and inclusiveness.

Respect

Respecting diverse cultures, individual differences, and environmental sustainability.

Our Projects

Project 1: Museum

Creating museums that blend innovative design with cultural heritage, showcasing the richness of Canadian and global traditions.

Project 2: Community Centre

Developing inclusive community centers to foster social engagement and provide spaces for education, art, and well-being.

Project 3: Art Gallery

Designing world-class art galleries that celebrate creativity and provide platforms for emerging and established artists.
